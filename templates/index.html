Trust Model

TrustedUX is based on trust model that measures trust factor on four aspects.

Risk perception
defined as the extent to which one party is willing to participate in a given action while considering the risk and incentives involved.
Benevolence
define as user 's perception that a particular system will act in their best interest and that most people using the system share similar social behaviors and values.
Competency
The ease of use associated with the use of a system in that it is perceived to perform its tasks accurately and correctly.
Reciprocity
the degree to which an individual sees oneself as a part of a group. It is built on the principle of mutual benefit, feeling a sense of belonging and feeling connected

Use-Case scenario

TrustedUX can be used to measure trust in five types of scenarios.

Individual
You have a technical product and you want to measure trust for it. In this case, individual case can be used.
Compare A & B
Either you have two products to compare on trust scale or you want to measure trust for your product in two situation (before/after). This case will suit the need.
Compare A, B and C
Either you have three products to compare on trust scale or you want to measure trust for your product in three situations. This case will suit the need.
Compare A, B, C and D
Either you have four products to compare on trust scale or you want to measure trust for your product in four situations. This case will suit the need.
Compare A, B, C, D and E
Either you have five products to compare on trust scale or you want to measure trust for your product in five situations. This case will suit the need.
